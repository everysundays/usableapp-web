## Section 3: How It Works

### Section Headline
**Big Bold Text**: "Refine the problem. Define the solution."

### Content

Every product is different. Every business has unique patterns. We don't rush discovery‚Äîwe do it right.

**The Process:**
- As many proof-of-concepts as needed (typically 5-7 maximum)
- Each PoC validates one assumption, tests one unknown
- Research-driven, user-validated methodology
- Time-based contract ensures thoroughness

**Our Methodology:**
[Product Surface Foundation diagram visualization]

Starting from your business foundation, we layer discovery:
- Problem Statement ‚Üí What are we really solving?
- Goal & Expectation ‚Üí What does success look like?
- User Requirements ‚Üí What do users actually need?
- Workflow ‚Üí How will this actually work?
- Data Foundation ‚Üí What information drives decisions?

Each layer builds on validated insights, not assumptions.
