# Content Strategy

## Target Audience
- **Primary**: Startup founders
- **Secondary**: SME decision-makers (2nd/3rd generation, newly established)
- **Psychographic**: Leaders wanting to try new things, innovation-focused, and they think it can solve their problems
- **Geographic**: Bangkok-based with potential international reach

## Tone & Voice
- **Style**: Witty, catchy, but professional and credible
- **Level**: Philosophical (like "Behind Technology There's Human")
- **Constraint**: Clear enough that customers understand the value and see it as worth their money/time
- **Goal**: Position as strategic consultants, not just executors

## Site Sections (Single Page)

### 1. Hero
- Bold, one-sentence statement (see explorations above)
- Visual hierarchy: statement dominates
- Brief but clear enough to intrigue

### 2. What We Do
Three-step value proposition:
1. **Foundation Discovery** - Refine problem statements, discover goals & expectations
2. **PoC & Research** - Plan research, conduct interviews, validate with real end-users
3. **Deliverables** - First prototype sketch + firm Business Requirement Specifications (BRS) with KPIs

Note: "We use Generative AI for documentation and drafting, but never for thinking."

### 3. How It Works
- Brief but clear methodology explanation
- Show process count (normally 5-7 PoCs max)
- Visual: Product Surface Foundation diagram (core methodology)
- Goal: Create questions that lead to conversations

### 4. Showcase
- 2 project case studies (placeholders for now)
- Focus on results and methodology application

### 5. Pricing/Engagement Model
- **Meeting 1**: Free brief session with client (NDA available)
- **Meeting 2**: Proposal from Usable App (working plan from discovery to BRS)
- **Project Structure**: Time contract basis
- **Timeline**: Depends on discovery process (normally 5-7 PoCs max)
- **Output**: BRS that serves as guidelines for product development

### 6. Contact/CTA
- **Primary CTA**: "Start your app's day-1 with us" (or variation)
- **Interaction**: Business card flip effect
  - **Front**: Illustration (visual identity)
  - **Back**: Contact information (revealed on click/hover)
  - **Purpose**: Track user engagement, hide from bots
- **Location**: 36 Thawiwattana 47, Thawiwattana, Bangkok 10170
- **Phone**: +66 61 831 7773
- **Method**: Email/phone direct contact (no form)
- **Tracking**: Button click events to measure interest

## Big Bold Text Concept
Each fold should have one clear, bold sentence that captures the main idea. These should be visually dominant and guide the narrative flow.

## Key Messaging Points
- Strategic product discovery, not just execution
- Business patterns reveal product needs
- Research-driven, user-validated approach
- AI-assisted but human-driven thinking
- Methodology-backed consulting
- Clear deliverables (prototype + BRS with KPIs)
