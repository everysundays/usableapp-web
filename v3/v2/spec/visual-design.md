# Visual Design Direction

## Design Philosophy
- **Approach**: Plain but bold
- **Constraint**: NO copying existing palettes or trained data patterns
- **Goal**: Create fresh, original visual identity

## Typography

### Primary Font: Anakotmai (Thai-optimized)
Available from: `/Users/Lh/Sites/_UA/fonts/anakotmai.css`

**Weights Available**:
- Light (300) - `anakotmai-light.woff2`
- Medium (400) - `anakotmai-medium.woff2`
- Bold (700) - `anakotmai-bold.woff2`

**Usage Strategy**:
- **Bold (700)**: Hero statements, section headlines (the "big bold text")
- **Medium (400)**: Body copy, descriptions
- **Light (300)**: Subtext, captions, metadata

**Characteristics**:
- Thai language optimized but works for English
- Modern, clean, professional
- Good readability for both short headlines and longer text

## Color Palette Exploration

### Reference Inspiration
**iO Digital (iodigital.com)**: Clean minimalist aesthetic, neutral understated colors, white background with grayscale and subtle accents. Professional yet modern.

### What to AVOID from v1:
- Cream/sand/terracotta earth tones (usableapp-cream, sand, clay, sage, terracotta)
- Heavy color systems (6+ colors)
- Predictable "corporate" palettes

### New Direction - Concept-Based Palettes:

**Concept 1: Human × Usability × Digital Product**
*Warmth meets clarity meets precision*
- **Base**: Warm white/cream (#FAFAF8) - human touch
- **Primary**: Soft charcoal (#3A3A3C) - readable, approachable
- **Accent 1**: Coral/salmon (#FF6B5B) - human warmth, usability focus
- **Accent 2**: Steel blue (#5B7C99) - digital reliability
- **Highlight**: Bright amber (#FFAB40) - action/discovery moments
- **Philosophy**: Combines human-centered warmth with digital precision

**Concept 2: Human × Unconventional × Problem Solver**
*Breaking patterns to solve problems*
- **Base**: Pure white (#FFFFFF) - clean slate
- **Primary**: Deep purple (#6B2C91) - unconventional, creative thinking
- **Secondary**: Graphite (#2D2D2D) - serious problem-solving
- **Accent**: Electric lime (#CDDC39) - unexpected solutions
- **Support**: Warm gray (#8E8E8E) - balance
- **Philosophy**: Bold, unexpected combinations that challenge conventions

**Concept 3: Digital × Human × Understanding**
*Technology that understands people*
- **Base**: Cool light gray (#F5F7F9) - digital clarity
- **Primary**: Navy (#1A3A52) - trustworthy digital
- **Accent 1**: Warm terracotta (#D07855) - human understanding
- **Accent 2**: Soft cyan (#64B5F6) - clear communication
- **Text**: Near-black (#1C1C1E) - maximum readability
- **Philosophy**: Bridges digital capability with human empathy

**Concept 4: Ease of Use × Human × Problem Solver**
*Simplifying complexity for humans*
- **Base**: Off-white (#FCFCFC) - effortless backdrop
- **Primary**: Forest green (#2D5A3D) - growth, problem-solving
- **Secondary**: Warm black (#2A2A2A) - grounded, confident
- **Accent**: Soft gold (#E6B84A) - ease, value, clarity
- **Support**: Sand (#C9B8A3) - approachable, warm
- **Philosophy**: Natural, approachable colors that reduce cognitive load

### Recommended:
**Concept 1** (Human × Usability × Digital) or **Concept 3** (Digital × Human × Understanding) - Both balance professional credibility with human warmth

## Layout & Visual Elements

### Hero Section
- Full viewport height option
- Typography-dominant (text is the visual)
- Optional: subtle tint/overlay if video background used
- No heavy animations, just smooth scrolling

### Section Structure
- Clear fold divisions
- One big bold statement per section
- Generous whitespace
- Content hierarchy through scale, not decoration

### Methodology Visualization
- **Product Surface Foundation Diagram** (from 306994_0.jpg)
- Must visualize the layered foundation concept
- Show: Bottom layer (Foundation) → Communication → Problem Statement → Goal & Expectation → Data Foundation → Workflow → etc.
- Consider: Interactive or static infographic
- Style: Clean, architectural, process-focused

### Anti-patterns to Avoid
- Video backgrounds (unless subtle and purposeful)
- Heavy scroll animations
- Parallax effects
- Over-decorated UI elements
- Busy/cluttered layouts

## Responsive Approach
- Mobile-first design
- Typography scales down but stays bold
- Diagram simplifies for mobile (possibly stepwise vs layered)
- Touch-friendly CTAs (minimum 44x44px)
