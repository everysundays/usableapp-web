# Color System - 60-30-10 Ratio

## Color Palette (Concept 3)

### 60% - Base/Dominant
**Purpose**: Backgrounds, containers, large areas
- **Base Gray**: `#F5F7F9` - Main background
- **White**: `#FFFFFF` - Alternating sections

**Usage**:
- Hero: White background
- What We Do: Base gray background
- How It Works: White background (blueprint exception)
- Showcase: Base gray background
- Pricing: White background
- Contact: Base gray background

---

### 30% - Secondary/Supporting
**Purpose**: Headings, important text, structural elements
- **Primary Navy**: `#1A3A52` - Main brand color
- **Primary Light**: `#2C5273` - Hover states
- **Primary Dark**: `#0F2336` - Deep backgrounds

**Usage**:
- All body text and paragraph content
- Section headings (with color variations)
- Navigation elements
- Footer background
- Card borders and structure

---

### 10% - Accent/Emphasis
**Purpose**: CTAs, highlights, important actions
- **Terracotta**: `#D07855` - Warm, human accent
- **Cyan**: `#64B5F6` - Cool, digital accent

**Usage**:
- Step numbers in What We Do
- Links and interactive elements
- Button hover states
- Diagram accents
- Call-to-action buttons

---

## Section Color Functions

### Hero
- **Background**: White (60%)
- **Heading**: Primary Navy (30%)
- **Subtext**: Text Light

### What We Do
- **Background**: Base Gray (60%)
- **Heading**: Primary Navy (30%)

- **Background**: White (60%)

- **Heading**: Terracotta (10% - accent for variation)
- **Cards**: White backgrounds
- **Meta Labels**: Terracotta (10%)

### Pricing
- **Background**: White (60%)
- **Heading**: Primary Navy (30%)
- **Badges**: Terracotta (10%)
- **Highlight Card**: Primary Navy background

### Contact
- **Background**: Base Gray (60%)
- **Heading**: Primary Navy (30%)
- **Card Front**: White

- **Button**: Terracotta (10%)

## Color Distribution Summary

### By Percentage:
- **60% Base**: #F5F7F9 (gray) + #FFFFFF (white) = backgrounds
- **30% Primary**: #1A3A52 (navy) = text, headings, structure
- **10% Accents**: #D07855 (terracotta) + #64B5F6 (cyan) = highlights, CTAs

### By Function:
- **Navy (#1A3A52)**: Trust, professionalism, digital expertise
- **Terracotta (#D07855)**: Human understanding, warmth, discovery
- **Cyan (#64B5F6)**: Communication, clarity, process

---

## Color Don'ts (Mixing Prevention)

❌ **Don't** use Terracotta for structural elements
❌ **Don't** use Cyan for large backgrounds
❌ **Don't** mix warm (Terracotta) and cool (Cyan) in the same small element
❌ **Don't** use Navy for small accent elements
❌ **Don't** use accent colors for more than 10% of any section

✅ **Do** keep backgrounds neutral (60%)
✅ **Do** use Navy for all major text and structure (30%)
✅ **Do** reserve accents for highlights and CTAs (10%)
✅ **Do** maintain contrast ratios for accessibility

---

## Accessibility

All color combinations meet WCAG AA standards:
- Navy on White: 12.6:1 (AAA)
- White on Navy: 12.6:1 (AAA)
- Terracotta on White: 4.7:1 (AA)
- Cyan on White: 3.1:1 (AA Large Text)
- White on Primary Dark: 15.8:1 (AAA)
