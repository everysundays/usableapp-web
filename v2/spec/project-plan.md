## Risk Management

### Potential Delays
1. **Copy approval**: Could take longer than expected
   - **Mitigation**: Start with placeholder copy, continue dev work

2. **Illustration creation**: Custom assets take time
   - **Mitigation**: Use placeholders initially, swap later

3. **Client availability**: Review cycles could extend
   - **Mitigation**: Async reviews, clear decision deadlines

### Buffer Time
Built-in 2-3 day buffer before November 5 launch for unexpected issues
